About


An experienced educational leader with over 30 years of dedicated service, Ms. Vandana Saxena is the Principal of Learning Paths School, where she champions academic excellence and holistic student development. Throughout her distinguished career, she has consistently demonstrated a passion for creating inclusive, future-ready learning environments that not only focus on academic achievement but also nurture emotional well-being and mental health resilience.

A firm believer in the power of collaboration, she actively engages with students, parents, and educators to build strong partnerships that enrich the educational experience. Her expertise spans curriculum development, educational innovation, and the integration of sustainable practices within school systems.

Beyond her role as Principal, she extends her commitment to education through her YouTube channel — Parenting Ki Pathshala — where she shares valuable insights on parenting, child psychology, and practical strategies to support children’s academic and personal growth. The channel reflects her vision of empowering parents and educators to work together in shaping confident, balanced, and compassionate learners.
